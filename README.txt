Requirements: Symphony version 2.0.3 or later.


[INSTALLATION]

1. Upload the 'ysboss' folder in this archive to your Symphony 'extensions' folder.

3. Enable it on System > Extensions page: select the "Yahoo! Search BOSS",
   choose Enable from the "With selected..." menu, then click "Apply" button.

4. Go to System > YSBOSS and configure options there. For additional instructions click "help" button.

5. Say big thanks to Yahoo! by putting their logo on your site :).
